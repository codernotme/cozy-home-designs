
# NC - Furniture & Kitchen Equipment Website

A clean, modern, mobile-responsive website showcasing both furniture and kitchen/bakery equipment products with an inquiry-based catalog.

## Design Style
- Inspired by the uploaded reference image: warm tones, clean layout, elegant typography
- Neutral color palette (beige, warm grays, soft blacks) with accent colors
- Modern card-based product layouts with high-quality image placeholders

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with a large lifestyle banner image and tagline
- **Category navigation** chips/tabs (Furniture, Kitchen Equipment, Bakery Equipment)
- **Featured products** grid showcasing top items from both categories
- **About preview** section with company highlights
- **Testimonials** carousel from satisfied customers
- **Call-to-action** banner encouraging inquiries

### 2. About Page
- Company story and mission (NC brand, based in Kanpur)
- Manufacturing capabilities overview
- GSTIN and business credentials
- Team or facility highlights

### 3. Products Page
- **Filterable product catalog** with categories:
  - Furniture (sofas, chairs, tables, lighting, d√©cor)
  - Kitchen Equipment (chapati machines, steam cooking plants, roti machines)
  - Bakery Equipment
- Product cards with image, name, brief description, and "Inquire" button
- Category sidebar/filter bar for easy browsing

### 4. Product Detail Pages
- Large product images
- Specifications table (size, capacity, materials, etc.)
- Description and features
- "Request Quote" / inquiry button

### 5. Gallery Page
- Masonry or grid photo gallery
- Filter by category (Furniture / Kitchen Equipment)
- Lightbox for full-size image viewing

### 6. Testimonials Page
- Customer reviews with names and photos
- Star ratings
- Organized by product category

### 7. Contact Page
- **Inquiry form** with fields: name, email, phone, product interest, message
- Company details: address (Kanpur), email (Newcoolsindia@gmail.com), phone numbers
- Embedded map showing location
- WhatsApp quick contact button

## Global Components
- **Header**: Logo, navigation menu, search bar, mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer**: Quick links, contact info, social media icons, GSTIN info
- **Mobile**: Fully responsive with bottom navigation or collapsible menu

## Data Approach
- All product data stored in local JSON/TypeScript files (no backend needed initially)
- Contact form will show a success message (can be connected to email service later)
